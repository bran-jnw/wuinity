The Modelling Tool simulates human and fire behavior during a wildfire evacuation at the wildland-urban interface (WUI) and represents a way to enhancing situational awareness of Authorities Having Jurisdiction (“AHJs”) as they plan and train for a potential WUI scenario.  This tool and any related suggestions for mitigation measures are not designed to replace or substitute an AHJ’s decision about evacuation during a wildfire.
